Privacy Policy for SpecScan VIN Decoder

Last Updated: October 22, 2025

1. Introduction

SpecScan VIN Decoder ("we," "our," or "us") operates the SpecScan mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

Account Information:

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Profile information you choose to provide

Vehicle Information:

  • Vehicle Identification Numbers (VINs) you enter or scan
  • Vehicle specifications and decoded data
  • Search history within the App
  • Saved vehicles and favorites

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers (Android ID, Advertising ID)
  • App version
  • Mobile network information

Usage Data:

  • App features accessed
  • Time and date of App usage
  • Session duration
  • Interaction with App features
  • Error logs and crash reports

Camera Permissions:

  • When you use the VIN scanning feature, we access your device camera
  • Camera images are processed locally on your device
  • We do not store camera images or photos

2.3 Information from Third Parties

Base44 Vehicle Data API: We use Base44 services to decode VIN information. Vehicle specifications, history, and technical data are retrieved from Base44, which may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

3.1 Core App Functions

  • To provide and maintain VIN decoding services
  • To process and display vehicle information
  • To save your search history and preferences
  • To authenticate your account and manage access
  • To enable App features you request

3.2 Communication

  • To send service-related notifications
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • To provide updates about the App
  • To send promotional communications (with your consent)

3.3 Improvement and Development

  • To analyze App usage and improve functionality
  • To develop new features and services
  • To diagnose technical issues and bugs
  • To perform research and analysis

4.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We share information with service providers who perform services on our behalf:

  • Base44 (Vehicle Data Provider): VINs you submit for decoding, account identifiers, and usage data

  • Analytics Providers: Aggregated and anonymized usage statistics

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to court orders, government requests, or to protect our rights and safety.

4.3 Aggregated Data

We may share aggregated or de-identified information that cannot reasonably be used to identify you.

5. Data Retention

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at support@specscan.net. We will respond within 30 days.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
